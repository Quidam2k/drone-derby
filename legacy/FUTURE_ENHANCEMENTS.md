## 🎮 **Long-term Vision**

Drone Derby aims to become the premier platform for strategic programming games, combining the tactical depth of classic board games with modern technology and social features. The ultimate goal is to create a sustainable, growing community of players who enjoy both competitive play and creative board design.

### **5-Year Goals**
- 1 million registered users
- 100,000+ community-created boards
- Regular tournament scene with sponsorships
- Educational adoption in schools worldwide
- Recognition as a leading strategy game platform

### **10-Year Vision**
- Drone Derby as an esports phenomenon
- AI research platform for game theory
- VR/AR standard for board game visualization
- Global community with regional championships
- Educational curriculum integration worldwide

This roadmap provides a comprehensive view of potential improvements and innovations that could be implemented as the project grows and matures. Each enhancement should be evaluated based on user feedback, technical feasibility, and business value.
